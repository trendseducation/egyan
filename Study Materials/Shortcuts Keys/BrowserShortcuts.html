Browser Tips

Sync Across Devices

Sign in to your browser account to sync bookmarks, passwords, and history across all devices.

Security First

Use Ctrl+Shift+Delete to clear browsing data. Enable automatic updates for security patches.

Performance Tips

Close unused tabs, disable unnecessary extensions, and clear cache regularly for better performance.

Mobile Browsing

On mobile, use gestures like swipe to go back/forward and pull to refresh for better navigation.
