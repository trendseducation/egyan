Quick Tips for GST in TallyPrime

GST Reconciliation

Use Alt+R to quickly access GST reconciliation reports and match purchase data with GSTR-2A/2B.

Quick GST Invoice

Press Alt+G in sales voucher to instantly convert it to a GST invoice with all required details.

Export GST Returns

Use Alt+X to export GST return data for offline filing or backup purposes.
