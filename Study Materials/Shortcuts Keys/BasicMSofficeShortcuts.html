Pro Tip: Master Navigation Shortcuts

Using Ctrl+G (Go To) in Excel/Word or F5 in PowerPoint can dramatically reduce time spent scrolling through large documents. Combine with Ctrl+Arrow Keys for rapid navigation.

Efficiency Analysis

Learning Excel's Alt+= (AutoSum) and Ctrl+Shift+L (Toggle Filters) can save data analysts up to 15 minutes daily. For frequent presenters, PowerPoint's Shift+F5 (Start from current slide) eliminates presentation fumbling.

Hidden Gems

Use Ctrl+Shift+C/V to copy/paste formatting across Office apps. In Outlook, Ctrl+Shift+M creates a new email, and Ctrl+R replies instantly. These shortcuts are consistent but often underutilized.
